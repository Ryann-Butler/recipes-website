Cheesy Lasagna Sheet Pasta

Home

This 3 ingredient cheesy lasagna sheet pasta could not be easier Serve topped with
Parmesan cheese, red pepper flakes, and freshly cracked black pepper if you like.



Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total Time: 20 mins
Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces lasagna noodles, broken in half
  • 1 jar tomato sauce
  • 8 ounces shredded whole milk mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water,
    stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 10 to 12 minutes.
  2. Strain noodles and return to the pot. Add tomato sauce and cook on medium heat until sauce is heated through, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add cheese and stir until cheese is melted. Serve immediately.
